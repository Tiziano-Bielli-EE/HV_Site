ABOUT ME:


I was born in Italy, Lombardy in 2001. Right from being young I would tear apart things to learn how they were working.
During secondary school, after recovering some analog instruments from a scrapyard, I built my first "AC monitoring box", and since then, I've been learning and experimenting with high voltage, electric traction and electrical measurements.
I am currently studying electrical enginering at Milan. In my spare time, I like to work on my projects.
